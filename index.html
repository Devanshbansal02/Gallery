My first addiction




I was only 8 years old when my infatuation with the rubik's cube started. I started with a simple blocky cube when I first started, and believe my experience was not the best. Eventually this infatuation was buried under the pile of technologies that followed behind it. My real journey began when I was 16 and solved the cube for the first time. As I kept practicing I became faster and the boy who once gave up on solving 3x3 nowadays solves 7x7 as timepass.
